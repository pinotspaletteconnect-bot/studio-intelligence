# Studio Intelligence Roadmap

**Version 3.0**

**Last Updated:** July 9, 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines the long-term development roadmap for Studio Intelligence.

Unlike the **Current Status** document, which tracks active development, this roadmap describes the major platform capabilities that Studio Intelligence will deliver over time.

Roadmap phases represent **business capabilities**, not individual software tasks.

---

# Platform Evolution

Studio Intelligence is evolving through five major stages.

```text
Infrastructure

↓

Data Collection

↓

Business Intelligence

↓

Artificial Intelligence

↓

Business Automation
```

Each phase builds upon the previous one.

---

# Cross-Platform Initiatives

These initiatives span multiple roadmap phases.

## Multi-Tenant Platform

Support unlimited organizations, brands, and locations through configuration rather than custom development.

---

## Historical Data

Preserve complete historical records to support trend analysis, forecasting, benchmarking, and AI.

---

## AI Readiness

Ensure every business domain exposes standardized reporting views suitable for AI consumption.

AI should consume business-ready data rather than raw integration tables.

---

## Unified Intelligence

Continue integrating business domains into a single operating platform capable of providing cross-functional insights.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every roadmap initiative should support one or more of the following objectives:

* Increase revenue
* Improve profitability
* Reduce manual work
* Improve operational efficiency
* Enhance customer experience
* Enable data-driven decision making
* Prepare the platform for AI and automation

Projects that do not support these objectives should be carefully evaluated before implementation.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Studio Intelligence will evolve from a business intelligence platform into an intelligent business operating system.

Its ultimate purpose is to:

* Collect business data automatically.
* Organize information into a single source of truth.
* Explain business performance.
* Predict future outcomes.
* Recommend the best course of action.
* Automate routine operational decisions.

Success is achieved when business owners no longer spend time gathering information—they spend their time making better decisions powered by Studio Intelligence.
